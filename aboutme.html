About Me

Hello! I'm Son Vu, also known as Lucas. I'm a dedicated and versatile IT professional with a passion for technology, problem-solving, and creating meaningful solutions through software development. My journey in IT has been an exciting blend of self-driven exploration and hands-on experience, enhanced by a diverse background in both business and technology.

Background & Work Experience

With over two years of experience working in the IT industry, I've worn multiple hats across various projects. My career started in the Finance and Banking sector, where I worked for four years, gaining in-depth knowledge of financial processes and understanding the client's perspective. Following this, I moved into the Air Ticketing industry, spending two years contributing to projects focused on flight booking and reservation systems, which gave me valuable domain experience in travel technology. I’ve taken on roles that span from full-stack development to product management, which has allowed me to be actively involved in various facets of software development. For instance, I’ve worked as a Full-Stack Web Developer for E9pay, spearheading the development of a terminal application using a mix of technologies like Flask, Python, and JavaScript. I've also played a key role as Product Manager for the E9pay Mobile App, focusing on improving user experience and adding innovative features to make the app more competitive.

Technical Skills
